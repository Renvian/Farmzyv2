How Farmzy Works

Revolutionizing the agricultural supply chain through smart aggregation.

👨‍🌾

1. Empowering Farmers

We assign demand-based targets directly to farmers. They grow exactly what the market needs, reducing waste and ensuring guaranteed sales.

🔄

2. Smart Aggregation

Farmers submit their harvest quantities through our app. Our aggregation centers consolidate these into a unified inventory, ready for distribution.

🛒

3. Fresh to Consumers

Consumers get access to an always-fresh marketplace. Real-time freshness tracking ensures you know exactly the quality you're buying.

The Benefit

Traditional supply chains involve multiple middlemen, leading to price markups for consumers and low margins for farmers. Farmzy acts as a single, transparent aggregator. By predicting demand and managing harvest targets, we eliminate the bullwhip effect in agricultural supply chains.
